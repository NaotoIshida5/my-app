Exquisite Agu Pork & Carefully Crafted Broth

At Shabu-Shabu Yanagi, we take pride in serving premium Agu pork, a rare and highly sought-after breed. Our Agu pork is completely odorless, with fine marbling and a delicate sweetness. Paired with our specially crafted broth made from carefully selected ingredients, we bring out the pork’s natural umami to its fullest.

A Luxurious Dining Experience in an Intimate Setting

Our restaurant features an exclusive 9-seat counter, offering a sophisticated and private atmosphere. Whether dining alone, with a loved one, or for a business occasion, we provide the perfect setting for a truly memorable experience.

Finely Curated Sake & Exquisite Pairings

We offer a carefully curated selection of Awamori, Japanese sake, and fine wines, chosen to complement our dishes perfectly. Enjoy the harmony of flavors as you indulge in the art of food and drink pairing.

An Unforgettable Shabu-Shabu Experience

At Shabu-Shabu Yanagi, we offer a unique and extraordinary dining experience, combining the highest quality Agu pork, expertly crafted broth, and premium sake selections. We invite you to savor an unforgettable moment of culinary excellence with us.
